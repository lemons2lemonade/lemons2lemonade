About Me & News

I'm an Applied ML & Software Engineer with an M.S. in ECE (UW‑Madison) and B.S. in CS (UVA) based in Baltimore. I enjoy building resilient systems that leverage specialized data representations and hardware-aware optimizations to solve otherwise intractable problems.

Shaped UK IoT regulation — Co-author of SkillFence (Hooda, Wallace, et al., IMWUT 2022), the first practical defense against voice-assistant skill-confusion; cited in the UK Department for Science, Innovation & Technology's 2023 consumer-IoT harms review for the IoT Product Security Policy Team, supplying core evidence for the voice-assistant section of the "Secure-by-Design" enforcement strategy that underpins the Product Security & Telecommunications Infrastructure Act.
